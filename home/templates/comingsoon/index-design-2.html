What is Hairways Kenya?

Hairways Kenya is a platform that aims at bridging the gap between Salons, Barber-shops or Spas with their Clients.

For Salon owners, Hairways provides them with an online prescence for their businesses, it is like the Jumia or Kilimall of salons. It also provides them with a management system that helps them manage their financials, clients, appointments.

For clients, Hairways acts as your one stop platform to search for the best salons in your area, book salon appointments with your favourite salon, get beauty advice from the many beautitians that will be posting advices on beauty
